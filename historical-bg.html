History

The Historical Background. The Road Transport Employer's Association of Nigeria (RTEAN) Niger State Branch commenced operations in Niger State in 1982,under the leadership of a transporter and a car dealer Alhaji Muhammadu Etsu Gwari who is the pioneer chairman of RTEAN Niger State. His most remarkable legacies in his six year administration between (1982- 1989) were the spreading and edifying the union,protection of it's invation, manipulation and domination from the sister's unions. He was the owner of the only Mercedes Benz three seater that was used for town services in Minna town services in Minna township. He served for six years. In 1990, Alhaji Baba Dogara Bida took over. Even though there was not proper structural administration, he created three zones for operation which were; Minna zone,Bida zone and Agaie zones (1990-1996). He served for six years. Alhaji Saba Naira Dabban took up the chairmanship in 1997, he campaigned and enlightened the people about joining RTEAN, he encouraged the youths to join RTEAN which he succeeded in luring thousands of youth into the association between (1997- 2015). He served for eighteen years. Alhaji Abdullahi Dikko Manu was nominated to pilot the affairs of RTEAN as the next state chairman between(2015-2019). During his incumbency as the state chairman, a memorandum of understanding was signed between the three sisters union; NURTW, RTEAN and NARTO under the supervision of the then state Governor, Governor Sani Bello Lolo. That is the three sisters union has equal rights to operate in all parks and garages within the state capital and across the twenty five local government areas of Niger State. It was then that RTEAN begins to spread their tentacles to other places that will favour them. He spent four year serving. Alhaji Alfa Agaie took over in 2017 for the period of six months which gave room for nomination of Alhaji Garba Sule Paiko as the new state chairman between (2018-2020). Some of the legacies recorded were the creation of few chapels in some areas of the state, he was inaugurated as a hoot comitee on food distribution during Covid 19 pandemic by the then Governor Sani Bello of Niger State where he ensured that enough food stuffs were distributed to RTEAN members. Upon leaving the office he was appointed as a special committee by the National President to go round various states to checkmate ticket, Manifest,Uniforms and ensuring proper election conduction in some states affected. Presently,RTEAN in Niger State is chered and led by commerade, Alhaji Umar Salihu Katta. The youngest State Chairman,smart,vibrant and eloquent and visionary leader with a good forsight. He also serve as the National Auditor of the union. He is the special assistant to the present state Governor on youth mobilization. He is also the current Treasurer of Trade Union Congress (TUC) Niger State. He has a well constituted, structured Administration among others. In his quest to empower the youth around Niger State he expanded the chapels into sixty chapels cutting across all the twenty five Local Governments of the state. He also creat RTEAN Tricycle And Motorcycle Riders (RTEAMTAMR) in the state to ease transportation in the town. Other achievements includes; The purchase and reconstruction of RTEAN building in the heart of Minna the state capital, Leading the association to receive three car gifts from the state Governor, securing political appointments to some members of the association,provision of insurance policies,free medical treatments, acquisition of accident first aid kits,employing the administrative staffs to reduce unemployment and furnishing the salaries and welfare of the members. Alhaji Umar Salihu Katta has won and received several awards includes; Achiever of excellence/ epitome of good leadership, a member of National Institute of policy and strategic studies(certificate of attendance), Treasur Chapel A Minna.
