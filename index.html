Chairman's Address

This is the official website of Niger State Branch of Road Transport Employer's Association of Nigeria (RTEAN). I welcome you on board the train of RTEAN Niger State. Since 1982,we in Niger State have provided the Nigerlites with effecient transportation services to the public, both commercial and private which inturn have contributed immensely in boosting the state economy and the nation as a whole.
While we prioritized the safety and life of our people, checking the drug abuse and theft in our parks and garages, placing comfort to people. Our operation is duly backed by law. RTEAN in Niger State for the first time in 42 years is governed and operated in a refined and well structured Administration constituted during our service term. We have expanded our transportation services by creating a subsidiary of RTEAN Tricycle And Motorcycle Riders (RTEANTAMR) to magnify transportation services in the state.
The criteria for joining our dear association varies,for the driver's is the ownership of a vehicle while for the individuals,is the acquisition of membership which includes registration and purchasing of a membership card and identity cards. The rationale is that we ensure no alcoholism,theft,kidnapping and the use of fake RTEAN documents is curtailed so as not to endanger the lives and properties of people.
The RTEAN Marshal is created in the state to put that in check. And this is the more reason why we have enjoyed a very good relationship with the government ministries involved in transport sectors, the Niger State Governor and the State Government in general. We have also relished a mutual relationships with all the chief executives and their governments, the principal officers of the association and the State executive Council. We have also not forgotten our late past leaders and their families. This platform is created to provide you the necessary knowledge about us and our operations. In case you should require any information about us,that is not readily available here, kindly reach out to us through the below available contacts: 08035933158,09038000336,09165288888.

Yours Most Sincerely,
Alh. Umar Salihu Katta.
